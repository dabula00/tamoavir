Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12 June 2026

TAV ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the TAV mobile application.

By using TAV, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information — name, email address, date of birth, gender, and city when you register
  • Location data — your GPS location, only when the app is open, only to verify event attendance
  • Event check-ins — records of events you attend, including timestamp and venue
  • Reactions and ratings — your reactions to events and quality ratings you submit
  • Photos — photos you choose to share during live events, removed automatically when the event ends
  • Device information — push notification tokens for delivering event alerts you opt into
  • Usage data — screens visited and features used to improve the app

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To verify your physical presence at events you attend
  • To match you with events based on your preferences and attendance history
  • To provide venues with anonymous, aggregated audience intelligence
  • To send event notifications you have opted into
  • To calculate your VIP status at venues you visit regularly
  • To improve the TAV platform and user experience

3. Location Data

  • Location is only accessed while the app is open (foreground) or when you have confirmed attendance at an event (background, temporary)
  • Location is never tracked continuously or outside of active events
  • We never sell or share your precise location with third parties
  • Location access is optional — you can always check in by scanning the QR code at the venue entrance

4. How We Share Your Information

  • With venues you visit — your identity is shared with venues only in the context of your reviews at their events
  • Aggregated anonymous data — venues receive demographic summaries with no individual identification
  • Service providers — we use Supabase for secure data storage and authentication
  • Legal requirements — we may disclose information if required by law

5. Reviews and User Content

  • Reviews are displayed anonymously to other users — your name is never shown publicly
  • Venues can see your identity in the context of reviews about their own events only
  • Live event photos are visible only during the event and removed automatically when it closes

6. Your Rights

  • Access — view your personal data in your profile
  • Correct — update inaccurate data in your profile settings
  • Delete — permanently delete your account and all data via Settings → Delete My Account
  • Opt out — disable push notifications at any time in Settings → Notification Settings
  • Withdraw location — disable location access at any time in your device settings

7. Data Security

  • All data is stored securely with Row Level Security enabled — users can only access their own data
  • Authentication is handled with industry-standard encryption
  • No passwords are stored in plain text
  • No service-role keys are embedded in the app

8. Children's Privacy

TAV is intended for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18. Age is verified via date of birth during registration. Accounts found to belong to users under 18 will be removed.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app. The date at the top of this page shows when it was last updated.
